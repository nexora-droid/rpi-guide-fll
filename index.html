Raspberry Pi 5 Setup Guide

A complete beginner friendly guide from start to finish.

Before you begin

This guide assumes you have no knowledge of booting up a Raspberry Pi. If this is the case, follow along carefully. If you already have booted (and flashed) your RPI please skip to section 1.

1

Materials Required

Required

Essential components

1. Raspberry Pi 5

Available in 8GB or 16GB variants

2. MicroSD Card

Reccomended size is 128GB minimum. Project to be built uses around 100GB of data.

3. USB-C Power Supply

Official 27W USB-C power supply recommended (5V/5A)

4. MicroHDMI Cable

To connect to a monitor or TV (paired with a micro HDMI to HDMI adapter)

5. USB Cable and Mouse

For initial setup and navigation
